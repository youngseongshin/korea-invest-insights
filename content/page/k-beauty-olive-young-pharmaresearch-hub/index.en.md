## FAQ

### Can investors buy Olive Young shares?

Not directly. Olive Young is still private. The closest listed indirect exposure is CJ Corporation, while broader K-beauty exposure requires separating brands, ODMs, medical-aesthetics companies and device platforms.

### Is PharmaResearch a cosmetics company or a medical-aesthetics company?

PharmaResearch is closer to a PN/PDRN medical-aesthetics platform than a traditional cosmetics company. Rejuran is the visible brand; the core thesis is platform technology and expansion into regulated global aesthetics markets.

### Why put Olive Young and PharmaResearch in the same hub?

Both benefit from K-beauty globalization, but through different profit pools. Olive Young is a discovery and retail platform; PharmaResearch is a high-margin medical-aesthetics product platform. Together they map the consumer-to-clinic K-beauty value chain.
