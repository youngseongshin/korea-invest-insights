## Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-15

Korea Invest Insights publishes market commentary, research dashboards, and educational investor tools. This page explains what information may be collected when you visit this site and related subdomains, including `memory.koreainvestinsights.com`.

### Information We Collect

- **Basic visit data**: page views, referring pages, browser type, approximate geography, and timestamps may be collected through privacy-conscious analytics tools.
- **Technical logs**: hosting providers such as GitHub Pages and Cloudflare may process IP addresses, user agents, and request metadata to deliver and secure the site.
- **Comments and user-generated content**: blog comments may be handled through Giscus/GitHub. Memory Pulse comments may store a nickname, comment body, timestamp, language, page slug, and hashed IP/user-agent values for spam control. Raw IP addresses are not intentionally published.
- **Voluntary messages**: if you contact us through Telegram, Substack, GitHub, or another channel, we may process the information you choose to send.

### Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

The blog currently uses lightweight analytics, and the Memory Pulse dashboard uses Cloudflare Web Analytics and a public visit counter. These tools help us understand whether the site is useful and which pages need improvement.

If Google AdSense or another advertising partner is enabled, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to this site and other sites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.

### How We Use Information

We use information to:

- operate and secure the site,
- understand aggregate readership trends,
- prevent spam and abuse,
- improve dashboards, articles, and navigation,
- respond to voluntary messages or correction requests.

### Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by infrastructure and service providers that help operate the site, such as hosting, analytics, comments, newsletter, and anti-spam providers.

### User-Generated Content

Comments and public messages are the responsibility of their authors. We may remove comments that contain spam, promotional rooms, harassment, illegal content, or investment solicitations.

### Contact

For privacy questions, correction requests, or takedown requests, use the channels listed on the [Contact](/page/contact/) page.
