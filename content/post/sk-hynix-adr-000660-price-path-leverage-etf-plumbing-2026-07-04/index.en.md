> Context
> This is a follow-up to [SK Hynix F-1/A and F-6: how the SKHY ADR packages HBM capex](/post/sk-hynix-f1a-f6-skhy-adr-hbm-capex-2026-07-04/). The previous note focused on the filing mechanics. This note focuses on price transmission, ADR-common-share arbitrage and single-stock leveraged ETF flows.

## TL;DR

The SK Hynix ADR is structurally positive over the medium term, but it is not a near-term volatility dampener. It widens U.S. investor access and may allow SK Hynix to be compared more directly with U.S.-listed AI memory peers. But right after listing, the key variables are the offer discount, the first week of ADR trading quality, the ADR premium or discount to the KRX common share and the impact on Korea's single-stock leveraged ETF plumbing.

| Question | View |
|---|---|
| Is the ADR positive for 000660? | Medium-term yes, because investor access and peer framing improve. |
| Is it an immediate buy signal? | No. Pricing, discount and initial premium or discount must be checked first. |
| What is the key mechanism? | ADR-common-share arbitrage. If the ADR trades rich to implied parity, it can create KRX common-share buying pressure. If it trades cheap, it can pressure the common share. |
| Can the ADR absorb Korea's 2x ETF overheating? | Not in the short run. The bottleneck is KRX close rebalancing, not U.S. access. |
| Current stance | Watch the common share and the ADR. Avoid leveraged wrappers if the goal is HBM exposure. |

## 1. What Is Confirmed

SK Hynix has filed an F-1/A for a planned Nasdaq Global Select Market ADS listing under `SKHY`.[^sec-f1a] The F-6 filed by Citibank states that each ADS represents the right to receive one-tenth of one common share.[^sec-f6] Press reports indicate a maximum new-share issuance of 17.79 million common shares, around 2.5% of existing common shares.[^thebell]

The final ADS count, offer price and net proceeds remain blank in the F-1/A. That matters. The investment signal is not merely that an ADR is coming. The signal will be in the final price, the discount to the Korean common share and the first few days of trading.

## 2. Access Premium

The first effect is access. U.S. investors can buy SK Hynix exposure in dollars, during U.S. hours, without using a Korea trading account or indirect ETF exposure. That can shift the comparison set from “Korean memory stock” to “U.S.-tradable AI memory and HBM supplier.”

This is positive, but not new information for the stock. Much of the obvious ADR rerating argument has already been anticipated. The remaining alpha is whether U.S. investors pay at or above implied KRX parity after the ADR starts trading.

## 3. New Shares And Dilution

The ADR is linked to new share issuance, not merely a secondary sale by existing holders. The headline maximum is about 2.5% of existing shares. That is dilution in ownership terms, but not necessarily large economic dilution if the offer price is close to market and proceeds are invested into HBM, eSSD, advanced packaging and EUV-related capacity.

| Scenario | Read-through |
|---|---|
| Small discount and strong order book | U.S. investors validate the common-share price. Positive for 000660. |
| Large discount and weak demand | U.S. market rejects the prevailing valuation. Negative for 000660. |
| ADR premium after listing | U.S. demand can support KRX common shares. |
| Persistent ADR discount | U.S. trading becomes a new downside anchor. |

## 4. ADR-Common-Share Arbitrage

With one ADS representing 0.1 common share, parity is simple:

| Calculation | Formula |
|---|---|
| Implied ADR price from KRX common share | `KRX common price × 0.1 ÷ USD/KRW` |
| Implied common-share price from ADR | `ADR price × USD/KRW × 10` |

If the ADR trades above implied parity, arbitrageurs may buy KRX common shares and sell or create ADR exposure. That creates common-share buying pressure. If the ADR trades below parity, arbitrageurs may buy ADRs and sell common-share exposure, pressuring the KRX line.

Therefore the first week is not about the absolute ADR move. It is about the premium or discount versus the Korean common share, after conversion costs, FX, settlement and stock-borrow constraints.

## 5. U.S. Price Discovery

The ADR extends SK Hynix price discovery into U.S. trading hours. This helps incorporate Nvidia, Micron, SOX, rates and AI capex news faster. But it also imports U.S. overnight gap risk into the Korean open. The ADR is a price-discovery channel, not automatically a volatility cushion.

## 6. Leveraged ETF Plumbing

The ADR does not directly remove Korea's single-stock 2x ETF rebalancing pressure. Korean 2x ETFs must maintain daily target exposure through common shares, futures, swaps and liquidity provider hedging around the KRX close. The Korean Financial Services Commission described the daily 2x structure when single-stock leveraged products were introduced.[^fsc] The Korea Capital Market Institute estimated SK Hynix leveraged ETF AUM at KRW 9.15tn as of June 19 and highlighted how daily rebalancing can amplify volatility.[^kcmi]

The core issue is mechanical. When SK Hynix rises, 2x ETF exposure may need to increase. When it falls, exposure may need to decrease. That creates a short-gamma-like flow. An ADR only offsets this if U.S. long-only ADR demand is large enough to replace the domestic leveraged flow. That is possible later, but not proven now.

## 7. What To Watch

| Watch item | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Final offer price and discount | Tests U.S. demand quality. |
| First 3 to 5 days of ADR premium or discount | Determines whether U.S. trading supports or pressures 000660. |
| ADR trading value | Shows whether SKHY can become a real price-discovery line. |
| Korean leveraged ETF AUM | Shows whether the domestic short-gamma pipe is cooling. |
| HBM ASP, shipment and capex commentary | Keeps the ADR story grounded in earnings. |

## Final View

SKHY is a major capital-market event for SK Hynix. It can broaden the investor base and reframe the company as a U.S.-tradable AI memory supplier. But the ADR does not immediately solve the domestic leveraged ETF problem. The near-term stance is watchlist for the common share and ADR, and avoidance of leveraged wrappers if the goal is clean HBM exposure.
